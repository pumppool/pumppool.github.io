Why should you delegate to PUMP?

We are commited to the Cardano network regardless of price. HODL!

24/7 Uptime

We have created 2 reliable relay nodes to ensure maximum latency and expect to have 100% uptime for many years to come. All nodes have at least 8GB RAM, 160 SSD, and 4TB transfer to manage scalability with ease. We promise full transparency (regarding any fee, server, or update changes) to our delegators.

Distributed nodes

Our pool is run using three virtual private servers, two relays and one block producer distributed across different data centers in the United States and Europe. As a result, our pool will not miss a block if one relay becomes redundant (e.g. during maintenance/upgrades).

Maximum Security

Our nodes are expensive but we sacrifice cost for maximum efficiency and security. We use triple security measures (mobile authenticator, private key, and password) to secure all nodes. We charge the minimum fixed fee (340 ADA) and a 4% variable fee to our delegators to ensure the sustainability of our pool.

our timeline

Time isn't the only thing, it's the maing thing - Miles Davis

  • 2019

    Humble beginnings

    I initially became interested in cryptocurrencies as a means to promote freedom, great technology, and equality. As I continued to learn about various projects, it became clear that Cardano is here to stay and make changes for the better.

  • 2020

    An investment was made

    In early 2020 I put my money where my mouth is. As a student, it was hard to find capital but after luckily after investing all my savings in ADA, our team now has enough to fund our stakepool pledge.

  • 2021

    Creation of the PUMP pool

    This year I decided to create a stakepool to 1) help run the network, 2) become more involved in the Cardano community, and 3) increase decentralization. Creating the pool demonstrates our dedication to the protocol and will for it to succeed.

  • 2022 - future

    Commitment & dedication

    The future is bright for Cardano and we will remain committed despite haters and extremists. As Charles says, the dog keeps barking and the caravan keeps moving. Cardano will keep moving. The IOHK engineers are fantastic and we can't wait to see what unveils over the next few years.
