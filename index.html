Why should you delegate to PUMP?

We are commited to the Cardano network regardless of price. HODL!

24/7 Uptime

Alongside our block producer node, we have created 3 reliable relay nodes to ensure 100% uptime with minimum latency for many years to come. All nodes have at least 8GB RAM, 160 SSD, and 4TB transfer to manage scalability with ease. We promise immediate and full transparency (regarding any fee, server, or update changes) to our delegators.

Distributed nodes

Our pool is run using four virtual private machines, three relays and one block producer distributed across different data centers in the United States, Asia, and Europe. We keep 2 relays online 24/7 and have the third as a back up in case one relay becomes redundant (e.g. during maintenance/ upgrades). We do not miss staking rewards from the Cardano proof-of-stake protocol.

Maximum Security

Our nodes are expensive but they ensure maximum efficiency and security. We use triple security measures (mobile authenticator, private key, and password) to secure all nodes. If you are worried about losing ADA by staking, don't be, the tokens never leave your Yoroi or Daedalus wallet. There is a reason why >70% of circulating ADA is staked.

UPDATES

Consistent technical and pool-related updates.

As stake pool operators, we need to inform delegators of any changes that may occur to the pool. These include pool upgrades (if new code is released), protocol commits, or logistical changes. We want to remain as transparent as possible and ensure that our delegators become aware of updates ahead of time whenever possible. Regarding pool updates, we have not missed a node update. Regarding fee changes, there have been fraudulent pools which randomly change fees to the maximum (99% variable fee), but we can promise that no such thing will happen with us. We are here for sustainable growth and the long run. Any kind of update will be communicated ahead of time via the Telegram chat.

NEWS

We will share the latest Cardano news to keep delegators up to date.

Cardano has a lot going on all the time. From Hydra to the dapp store to the ERC-20 converter, a lot is happening. It is important to be up to date and we will provide interesting Cardano news updates via our Telegram chat and Youtube channel. As pool operators, we enjoy reading Github commits, IOHK releases, and Cardano-related Twitter pages and would like to better educate our delegators if there are knowledge gaps.

Giveaways

Giving back to our delegators is a priority.

At the moment, pool competition is extremely high as more and more pools are joining the network. As PUMP grows over time, we don't intend to change the fixed fee but will slowly grow the variable fee to a maximum of 1% as we want the best for our delegators. We also aim to give back to those who stuck around and helped us grow. For each pool milestone, our pool will have a giveaway to say thank you. Join the Telegram group for giveaway info!
